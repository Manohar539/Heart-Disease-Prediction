Comparative Interpretation

The three results must not be interpreted as a direct competition between identical prediction tasks. Each dataset represents a different cardiovascular outcome:

  • Framingham: ten-year coronary heart-disease risk.
  • Heart dataset: presence or absence of heart disease.
  • Heart Failure: adverse outcome during the recorded follow-up period.

For this reason, the project deploys three independent models through one integrated Flask platform rather than merging the targets into a medically unclear single outcome.

Academic notice: These metrics describe performance on the project test splits. They are not clinical validation results and should not be interpreted as evidence that the models are suitable for real medical decision-making.
